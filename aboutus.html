The coffees have engaging stories of farmers, pickers, wet mills and dry mills that are working hard to produce coffee with the highest quality. Our coffes are organic and fairtraide certified. We are working with Arabica which is referred to as a highland coffee, because it grows best at altitudes.

We work together with over 20 Finnish artisan roasters to bring you new, sustainable and delicious experiences. All of our coffees are roasted to order to ensure the best flavour.

Our mission is to bring these coffees to our costumers and complete the coffees story. This is shown not only through the expertly crafted cup but with the people and space that make up our shop.
